ABOUT ME


Markaelie is here. I'm a developer of interactive applications. I was born in 1991 at the tender age of zero. In the first half of the 90s, I got a 8bit Famicom console with an old black and white Soviet made TV Junost 406. The first experience of operating an interactive application with controllers made me a little crazy. It was a piece of paradise for me. A few years later I got my first computer Dell Inspiron 7000, it was a budget laptop for that time, released in 1997. The age of Windows PC has arrived.I was soon well versed in computer games, but the feeling of dissatisfaction still remained. I was interested in the fact how those games were developed/programmed. My interest in the digital world grew more and more. A little later I found a disc with 3dsmax 5. It was like a new dimension. Professional time has begun for me. Gradually, I learned to deal with my mistakes. With years of experience, I have come closer and closer to my specialization, following the idea that all amazing things are created by the energy of passion.
