About me

Hey! I am Mark Gerar Hunanyan, aka Markulie, an interactive developer and digital artist. I was born in 1991 at the tender age of zero. One of my most cherished childhood memories was getting an 8 bit Famicom console in the early 90s. The feeling of excitement was beyond words as I connected it to an old black and white TV and controlled interactive applications (8bit games) using the joystick for the very first time. It was as if I had stumbled upon my own personal paradise, and the pure joy I felt was nothing short of exhilarating!

But that was just the beginning. As I got older, I got my first computer, a Dell Inspiron 7000. It was a budget laptop for its time, released in 1997, but it marked the arrival of the age of Personal Computer. I was fascinated by how PC games and programs were programmed, and my interest in the digital world grew more and more.

Then, one day, I stumbled upon a disc with 3dsmax 5. It was a game-changer for me. It was like a new dimension had opened up, and I knew I wanted to pursue it professionally. Over the years, I learned to deal with my mistakes and honed my skills.

I have come to fully embrace the notion that the energy of passion is the driving force behind all amazing accomplishments. Whether it is creating a work of art or achieving a lofty goal, the fire of passion is what fuels our efforts and propels us forward. It is through our dedication to our passions that we are able to manifest greatness and bring our wildest dreams to life.
