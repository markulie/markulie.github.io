History

"Light of Life" is a deeply personal artwork, which I initially began in 2013. However, due to various reasons, I took a break from it for about a year. In March 2014, I recommenced the project armed with newfound skills and fresh enthusiasm. Leveraging the power of 3DS MAX 2014 and rendered with V-ray 3, I meticulously crafted every detail of this creation.

The entire creative process took approximately 1.5 months of dedicated effort, resulting in a captivating masterpiece comprising 412K polys (polygons). It embodies my artistic journey, growth, and dedication to the craft, culminating in an artwork that holds profound personal meaning to me.

After a few months, I decided to release my work on CGSOCIETY. To my surprise, Autodesk reached out to me and requested permission to use it as the official cover for their social networks, including Facebook and Twitter. Needless to say, I was overjoyed and honored by their request. It was truly gratifying to see my artwork recognized and appreciated by the developer of my favorite tool.

Granting them permission to use my artwork felt incredibly satisfying, as it validated the hard work and dedication I had poured into the project. Knowing that my creation would be featured on the platforms of a renowned company like Autodesk was a surreal and gratifying experience. It served as a testament to the passion and effort I had invested in "Light of Life" and encouraged me to continue honing my artistic skills.
