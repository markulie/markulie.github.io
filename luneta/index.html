Developer Notes

LUNETA is a special game for me, it's my very first developed game. It consists of 4 different games with unique gameplay. It's a bit mysterious, but once you understand the logic, you can enjoy it. I started developing LUNETA in 2016 for exclusicly iOS, it took about 2 years. I implemented several features in it ("Smooth Follow", Always on Screen Objects, Real Paralaxing, etc).
Try LUNETA's "Smooth Follow" feature from here: https://github.com/markaelie/SmoothFollow-Unity.
