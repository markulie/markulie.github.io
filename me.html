MARKULIE

Markulie is here. I'm an interactive/game/aesthetic developer. I was born in 1991 at the tender age of zero. In the first half of the 90s, I got a 8 bit Famicom console with an old black and white soviet made TV Junost 406. The first experience of controling interactive applications with joystick made me a little crazy. It was a piece of paradise for me. A few years later I got my first computer Dell Inspiron 7000, it was a budget laptop for that time, released in 1997. The age of Windows PC has arrived. I was interested in the fact how PC games were programmed. My interest in the digital world grew more and more. A little later I found a disc with 3dsmax 5. It was like a new dimension for me. Professional time has begun. Gradually, I learned to deal with my mistakes. With years of experience, I've come closer and closer to my specialization, following the idea that all amazing things are created by the energy of passion.
